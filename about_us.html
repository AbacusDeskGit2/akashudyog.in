About Us

We are the manufacturers and exporters of washing soap “5-Bhai”. We are dealing in the market since 1980’s. We offer best quality of washing soaps to wash any type of cloth.


We are considered as one of the reputed manufacturer, supplier and exporter of premium quality Washing Soap in India. Our Washing Soap is eco friendly and cost effective. As a result, we have the recommendation for manufacturing and supplying the Washing Soap without any animal ingredients like tallow etc.


Innovation is our forte and we strive to design and develop a precision engineered range of Washing Products. Our product range is Washing Soap. Processed under the hygienic conditions, this product is formulated using natural ingredients and is supplied under the brand name of 5 Bhai. Post-production, these are tested on various quality parameters, before delivering it to the customer's end. Also, these products are known for superior cleaning action, accurate compositions, effectiveness and longer shelf life.
